About Us


Welcome to Param Aanand


– Your Serene Escape at the Foothills of Mahuli, Nestled at the base of the majestic Mahuli Hills in
Shahapur, just a short drive from Mumbai, Param Aanand is a thoughtfully designed
family getaway and senior-friendly resort.
Surrounded by natural beauty, spiritual energy, and fresh mountain air, our resort offers the perfect setting
to relax, reconnect, and rejuvenate.
Whether you're looking for a peaceful retreat for your parents, a joyful family weekend, or a base for your
Mahuli trekking adventure, Param Aanand welcomes you with open arms and open skies.

What Makes Us Special
Senior-Friendly Comfort
We understand the needs of elderly guests, and our resort is built with accessibility, safety, and comfort at
its core. From barrier-free design to home-style food and calm, restful spaces — every detail is crafted with care.
Family-Focused Hospitality
With spacious accommodations, outdoor play areas, nature trails, and cozy gathering spots,
Param Aanand is designed for all ages to come together and create lasting memories.
Spiritually Enriched Setting
Just a stone’s throw away is *Manas Mandir*, one of the most revered Jain temples in the region.
Guests often speak of the calm and blessings they feel from being so close to this sacred space.
Adventure at Your Doorstep
Trekking enthusiasts will love our proximity to *Mahuli Fort*, one of the most iconic trekking destinations
near Mumbai. After an exhilarating hike, unwind in the peaceful embrace of nature.


AT PARAM AANAND
We blend Nature, Comfort, and Care* to create a truly unique experience. Whether you're seeking
Blessings, Bonding, or a Break from the city, you'll find it here – where every stay is filled with Peace, Joy, and Togetherness.
Come experience the bliss. Come home to Param Aanand.
